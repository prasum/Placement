About Us

The aim of the portal is to make students aware of the ongoing off campus and oncampus placements in the university.This portal should also serve as a stepping stone for all those budding engineers who wish to pursue their dream of higher studies by giving them access to all the necessary information relating to the competitive exams.This portal would be one of its kind model which would facilitate Alumni interaction along with which it would also serve as a platform where these alumni can notify about any opportunities they are willing to provide to the students of Thapar University.
